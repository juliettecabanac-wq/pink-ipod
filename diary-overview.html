Articles

These articles are mostly interviews with artists I genuinely love. I’m interested in giving smaller artists the same depth and intentionality we automatically grant to more established ones. Our conversations center around doubt, craft, childhood obsessions, failed projects, strange ambitions. I think these interviews are also an opportunity for them to sit with what they have done, usually for the first time. And, practically speaking, they’re more likely to answer my emails. Which helps. Welcome to this small archive !
